Overview

A luxury villa rental with a cook, Smugglers Nest is one of St Lucia's foremost holiday villas, enjoying a prime location on the north side of Soufriere Bay, with magnificent views of The Pitons.

The Local Area

Nestled in the heart of St Lucia's most picturesque region, Smugglers Nest offers easy access to pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and the iconic Pitons. Explore charming fishing villages, hike scenic trails, or indulge in water sports – there's something for everyone.
