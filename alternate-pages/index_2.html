Property Description

This stunning sophisticated villa designed by the Italian architect Paolo Piva is perfectly designed for entertaining inside and out. The large terraces and 20-metre pool are fantastic for lazing in the sun and enjoying the wonderful views of the ocean. Comprising a main residence with 6 bedrooms, there is also a separate guest cottage with its own features. Ideal for both families and parties.

Location

The villa is one of a handful of properties with views over Palmiste Bay. The location is one of the most private on the island, with a private path that leads directly to the white sand beach. The property is also within walking distance of the island’s only golf course, designed by Tom Fazio.

Interior

Designed with beautiful wood floors and open plan living, the main house has a large drawing room and a separate media room/den. The kitchen is state of the art and perfect for entertaining. The bedrooms are all air-conditioned with en-suite bathrooms and private verandas. There is also a separate guest cottage with its own pool.

Bedrooms

The property sleeps up to 14 guests in 7 en-suite bedrooms, six of which have air conditioning. The master bedroom has a king-sized four-poster bed, while the other rooms have a mix of king, queen, and twin beds. There is also a separate guest cottage with its own private pool and veranda.
